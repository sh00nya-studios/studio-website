1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account and does not knowingly collect your name, postal address, or other directly identifying information. The App does collect the following categories of information, primarily through third-party services described in Section 3:

1.1 Device and Advertising Identifiers

  • Advertising ID (e.g. Google Advertising ID / AAID) — used to serve and measure ads
  • Device type, operating system version, language, and general device settings
  • IP address (used transiently for regional ad delivery and fraud prevention; not stored by us directly)

1.2 Gameplay and Usage Data

  • In-app events such as level starts/completions, power-up usage, session length, and menu navigation
  • Aggregate progress data (e.g. current level, in-app currency balance) stored locally on your device and, where applicable, associated with an anonymous installation identifier

1.3 Diagnostic and Crash Data

  • Crash logs, stack traces, and device performance data collected automatically when the App encounters an error, used solely to identify and fix bugs

We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data, and the App contains no chat, messaging, or user-generated content features.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App's core gameplay and performance
  • To display and measure the effectiveness of advertising, including rewarded and interstitial ads
  • To diagnose technical issues and fix crashes
  • To understand aggregate usage trends (e.g. which levels players struggle with) so we can improve level design and difficulty balance
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

We do not sell your personal information. Where advertising partners process data for their own purposes (e.g. ad personalization), that processing is governed by their own privacy policies, linked below.
