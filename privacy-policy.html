Privacy Policy

App: Peel & Stack  ·  Effective date: July 14, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how sh00nya studios ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Peel & Stack mobile application (the "App"), available on Google Play.

This App is directed at, or expected to be accessed by, children. It is built and operated to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy Requirements — see Section 5. Before publishing, have this policy and your AdMob/Firebase configuration reviewed by someone familiar with COPPA compliance in your jurisdiction; the consequences of getting this wrong (FTC enforcement, Play Store removal) are more serious than for a general-audience app.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account and does not knowingly collect your name, postal address, or other directly identifying information. The App does collect the following categories of information, primarily through third-party services described in Section 3:

1.1 Device and Advertising Identifiers

  • Advertising ID (e.g. Google Advertising ID / AAID) — used to serve and measure ads for adult/general users only; not used for personalized or interest-based advertising to child users (see Section 5)
  • Device type, operating system version, language, and general device settings
  • IP address (used transiently for regional ad delivery and fraud prevention; not stored by us directly)

1.2 Gameplay and Usage Data

  • In-app events such as level starts/completions, power-up usage, session length, and menu navigation
  • Aggregate progress data (e.g. current level, in-app currency balance) stored locally on your device and, where applicable, associated with an anonymous installation identifier

1.3 Diagnostic and Crash Data

  • Crash logs, stack traces, and device performance data collected automatically when the App encounters an error, used solely to identify and fix bugs

We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data, and the App contains no chat, messaging, or user-generated content features.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App's core gameplay and performance
  • To display and measure the effectiveness of advertising, including rewarded and interstitial ads
  • To diagnose technical issues and fix crashes
  • To understand aggregate usage trends (e.g. which levels players struggle with) so we can improve level design and difficulty balance
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

We do not sell your personal information. Where advertising partners process data for their own purposes (e.g. ad personalization), that processing is governed by their own privacy policies, linked below.

4. Advertising and Your Choices

  • Because this App is directed at children, it is configured to serve only contextual, non-personalized advertising to all users, regardless of age — we do not rely on the standard adult consent-and-personalize flow for this App.
  • Our ad provider (Google AdMob) is configured with "child-directed treatment" (TFCD) and "under the age of consent" (TFUA) tags on every ad request, which instructs Google's ad systems not to use the advertising ID or any other identifier for interest-based or behavioral targeting.
  • Ad formats and the ad SDK used are limited to those certified under Google Play's Families Ads Program.
  • You can still reset your device's advertising identifier at any time: Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

5. Children's Privacy

Peel & Stack is directed at, or is likely to be accessed by, children under the age of 13, and we designed and operate it with that in mind. This section explains our practices under the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU/UK's child-data provisions (GDPR/UK GDPR "GDPR-K"), and Google Play's Families Policy Requirements.

5.1 What We Collect From Children

We do not require account creation and do not knowingly collect a child's name, email address, photo, or precise location. The App may collect limited technical and gameplay information described in Section 1 (device type, coarse/non-precise diagnostics, in-app event data such as level progress) strictly to operate the App, fix bugs, and measure aggregate, non-personalized ad delivery.

5.2 No Behavioral Advertising to Children

We do not use any identifier collected from a child to build an advertising profile, track a child across apps or websites, or serve personalized/interest-based ads. All ad requests from this App are tagged as child-directed (see Section 4), and our ad partner is contractually and technically restricted from using the data for any purpose other than serving a contextual ad and basic fraud/frequency protection.

5.3 Parental Rights

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information beyond what is described above, or you want to review, request deletion of, or refuse further collection of your child's information, contact us at the email in Section 10 and we will respond and take appropriate action, consistent with COPPA, without charging a fee.

5.4 Google Play Families Policy

This App is designed to comply with Google Play's Families Policy Requirements, including using only ad SDKs and analytics configurations certified for use in the Families program, and avoiding any feature (e.g. open-ended chat, external links without parental gates, or unrestricted web browsing) prohibited for apps in that category.

6. Data Retention

Gameplay progress stored locally on your device persists until you uninstall the App or clear its data. Aggregated analytics and crash data are retained by our service providers according to their own retention schedules, typically 14 months for Firebase Analytics event-level data, per Google's default retention setting.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (EEA/UK) or CCPA/CPRA (California) to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information, and to object to or opt out of certain data uses, including the "sale" or "sharing" of data for targeted advertising. Because the App does not collect directly identifying information, most requests are best exercised through your device's advertising and privacy settings (Section 4). For any other request, contact us using the details in Section 10.

8. International Data Transfers

Our third-party service providers (Google/Firebase/AdMob) may process and store data on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States, under their own applicable safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

9. Security

We rely on the security practices of our infrastructure and SDK providers (Google Play, Firebase, AdMob) and take reasonable measures to protect information within our control. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
